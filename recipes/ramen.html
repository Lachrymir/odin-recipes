Ramen

Description

It's like instant ramen, but not as lazy and way better.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 package ramen noodles with dried veggies
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. Combine broth and noodles into medium saucepan.
  2. Cover and boil on high heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes uncovered.
  5. Stir in sesame oil and add green onions
