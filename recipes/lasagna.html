Lasagna

Description

Simply some hearty, tasty lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 32oz Spaghetti Sauce
  • 32oz cottage cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese,divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Paramsean cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 Eggs
  • 9 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1/2 Water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the skillet until browned (8-10 mins). Drain grease, stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for about 5 mins.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, half of the parmasean cheese, dried parsley, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13 baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat for medium and top layers. Pour 1/2 cup water along edges of dish. Cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for 45 mins. Uncover and bake for an additional 10. Let cool for 10 mins.
